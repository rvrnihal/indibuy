# 🛒 IndiBuy – Industrial & Construction E-Commerce Platform  

IndiBuy is a specialized **e-commerce platform** designed for **industrial and construction product procurement**. It streamlines the process for industries, contractors, and suppliers by offering a reliable, easy-to-use, and scalable solution.  

---

## 🚀 Features  
- **Product Categories** – Browse through a wide range of industrial and construction items.  
- **Advanced Search & Filters** – Quickly find products based on category, price, and specifications.  
- **User-Friendly Navigation** – Clean, responsive, and intuitive UI for smooth browsing.  
- **Secure Checkout** – Safe payment gateways for hassle-free transactions.  
- **Order Tracking** – Real-time status updates on orders.  
- **Reliable Logistics** – Integration with trusted delivery partners.
